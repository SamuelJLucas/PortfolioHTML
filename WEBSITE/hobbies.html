Why I Like Books

Books are these wonderful, wonderful things to me. In a few ways. In one they are a tool for self growth
because we can reach into others' understandings through them. We don't have to build everything from the
beginning. It is insanely interesting to see people's points of view laid down on the page when, while
speaking it is so easy for meanings to fall away. Books are edited, read over, proofread, reviewed, and
the author can put as much work as they want into getting their idea across before you even see a single
piece of it. Their feelings, dreams, discoveries, there is so much to see.

It is very similar when reading novels. My favorite is the cozy genre. You can see the same amount of
who people are put into novels, but in the cozy genre it is the lightest and most loving areas of the person
that take center stage. Novels in this subgenre tend to cover heartwarming human actions and desires. It isn't
that there aren't tensions, twisted scenarios, or horrible things that happen. Rather, these things are all
there. The authors simply follow the flawed people that grow to be better.
